Singers Achivements and Award

Atif Aslam

Atif Aslam, a prominent Pakistani singer-songwriter and actor, has achieved significant recognition for his music, including multiple Lux Style Awards, Filmfare Awards, and the Tamgha-e-Imtiaz, a prestigious civilian honor from the Pakistani government.

Billie Elish

Billie Eilish's achievements include winning nine Grammy Awards, two Academy Awards (Oscars), two Golden Globe Awards, and numerous other accolades, solidifying her status as a global music icon and the youngest two-time Oscar winner.

Talha Anjumn

Talha Anjum, a Pakistani rapper, singer, and songwriter, is known for being a member and co-founder of the hip-hop band Young Stunners, and for his contributions to the Urdu hip-hop scene, including the PSL 6 anthem "Groove Mera".

Arijit Singh

Arijit Singh, a renowned Indian playback singer, has received numerous accolades, including five Mirchi Music Awards, seven Filmfare Awards, a Stardust Award, five IIFA Awards, five Zee Cine Awards, and four Screen Awards, showcasing his significant contributions to Indian music. He was also awarded the Padma Shri in 2025.
